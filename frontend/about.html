Reimagining the College Search

Propspetive college students often base their college search off of the reputation of the school, desired programs, career outcomes, etc. We often don't consider the mental health resources offered on campus. Mental health affects 73% of students during their time in college, leading to lower GPAs, higher drop out rates, and feelings of hopelessness. The Mental Health Initiative is determined to change the way students pursue their college search, making it easy for one to find data on their schools resources/student wellness in seconds.
